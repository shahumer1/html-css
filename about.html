About us

Kathy Ireland® Worldwide, is a 2-billion-dollar brand with a significant US footprint in furniture, home goods, fashion, and wellness products.


The next chapter for the brand expansion is to launch Kathy Ireland in UK and Europe. Our product portfolio for the UK will showcase an exciting collection of home products ranging from, Home Furnishing, Accessories, and latest style inspirations.
